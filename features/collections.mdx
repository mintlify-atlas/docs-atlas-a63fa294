---
title: "Collections"
description: "Save prompts to your personal collection and pin favourites to your public profile."
---

Collections let you bookmark prompts for quick access later. Your collection is private to your account; other users cannot see which prompts you have saved.

## Saving a prompt

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a prompt">
    Browse the library at [prompts.chat/prompts](https://prompts.chat/prompts) or open any prompt detail page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the bookmark button">
    On the prompt detail page, click the **Bookmark** (Save) button in the action bar. The button turns highlighted to indicate the prompt is in your collection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove from collection">
    Click the button again to remove the prompt. The button label changes to reflect the current state.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  You must be logged in to use collections. Clicking the bookmark button while logged out redirects you to `/login`.
</Info>

## Viewing your collection

Navigate to [prompts.chat/collection](https://prompts.chat/collection) to see all saved prompts sorted by the date you saved them (most recent first).

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Sorted by saved date">
    Prompts appear in the order you bookmarked them, with the most recently saved at the top.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Deleted prompts filtered out">
    If an author deletes a prompt you had saved, it is automatically excluded from your collection view.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Pinned prompts

Pinning is different from saving — pinned prompts appear as a highlighted section on your **public profile** page at `prompts.chat/@username`.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a prompt card">
    Hover over any prompt card. A **pin icon** appears in the top-right corner of the content preview.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the pin icon">
    Click the pin icon to pin the prompt to your profile. Pinned prompts are sorted by a custom `order` field so you can arrange them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unpin">
    Click the pin icon again on a currently-pinned prompt to remove it from your profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  You can pin up to **3 prompts** to your profile. The pin button is only visible on your own prompt cards when you are logged in. Visitors to your profile always see the pinned section.
</Info>

## Using the collection API

The collection is managed via two REST endpoints:

```bash
# Add a prompt to your collection
curl -X POST https://prompts.chat/api/collection \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{"promptId": "<prompt-id>"}'

# Remove a prompt from your collection
curl -X DELETE "https://prompts.chat/api/collection?promptId=<prompt-id>"
```

Both endpoints require an authenticated session cookie or API key.
